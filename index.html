Skills & Related Information

This section should show the most important information about your character, so limit each section to just a few items.

  • Primary Specialisations Your character should have no more than 3 primary specialisation. These are the things they are really focused on being the best at right now.

  • Additional Skills Again, only add a maximum of 3 additional skills, these are things that it's valuable to know about the character.

  • Languages Spoken If you're an Arcane Linguist, note that under Additional Skills and not Languages. Languages should be things that can be understood and spoken without needing magic.

  • Notable Mentors Please keep mentors to no more than three.

  • Additional Information Please keep additional information to only a few bullet points. If you find, when you generate the record, that some of your information has been cut off, please try to reduce your Additional Information section - or contact Kyandrial for help.
